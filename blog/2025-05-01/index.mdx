Vircadia World 0.0.1 is now available for local testing, feel free to try it out and give us feedback!

## Highlights

- **PostgreSQL-Powered Worlds**: Define your entire world in SQL with enterprise database features
- **Unified Architecture**: Bridge 3D assets and data across all platforms
- **Realtime State Tracking**: High-performance server-side tracking for all entity states
- **Developer-First Infrastructure**: Seamless development to production workflow
- **Apache 2.0 Licensed**: Full commercial freedom to modify and distribute

:::note

Vircadia World is in early developer alpha. If you need the production ready version earlier, please reach out to us at [hello@vircadia.com](mailto:hello@vircadia.com).
